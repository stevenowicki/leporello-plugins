## Template + rationale
Narrative-flow — chose because the story unfolds as a **sequence of discrete steps**, each with its own evidence and source. The visual reads as "step 1 → step 2 → step 3," not as a timeline (where dates matter) and not as a list (where order is incidental). In the v1 DLS the template stages **one step at a time** as a full broadcast frame — a giant hero step number, a headline-tier title, and a single line of context — with a first-class progress rail bottom-left; the presenter advances step-by-step.
